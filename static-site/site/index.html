How I work

Inspect. Map. De-risk. Execute. Verify. Document.

  1. Verify the environmentStart with observed state, not assumptions or stale diagrams.
  2. Map risk and dependenciesIdentify what must remain available, what is reversible, and what shares hidden dependencies.
  3. Make staged changesPrefer observable steps with explicit checkpoints and a known recovery path.
  4. Validate the business functionCheck service health, identity, data, applications, backups, and user outcomes.
  5. Leave a usable recordDocument findings, decisions, residual risk, and the next safe action.
