Current build

A persistent Network Assistant

I want an assistant that can hold a source-of-truth view of infrastructure, correlate telemetry and prior incidents, explain likely failure paths, recommend fixes with evidence, and verify recovery.

I’m building it around read-only infrastructure access, durable observations, source-backed knowledge, and human-controlled remediation.

The boundary I care about

  • Read-only access to production systems
  • Write access only to its own observations and knowledge
  • No production credentials capable of remediation
  • Evidence and confidence attached to recommendations
  • Independent detection when the AI layer fails

How I use the lab

Experiment, verify, keep the lesson

Build enough of the real system

I assemble the components needed to test integration, behavior, and recovery directly.

Break the assumption

I look for weak boundaries, failure behavior, recovery gaps, and the parts that are harder to operate than the diagram suggests.

Keep an honest record

I document the decision, evidence, limits, and next experiment so the work can compound.

I intentionally leave credentials, internal addresses, administrative entry points, private data, and sensitive operational details out of public lab material.
