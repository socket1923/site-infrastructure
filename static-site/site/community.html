Dusty Angels

Dusty Angels began as an idea for helping home-lab operators, self-hosters, and qualified community organizations improve security through practical assessment and knowledge sharing.

The initiative is not currently presented as an open service program. Any future version will publish its scope, authorization model, disclosure process, and availability before accepting participants.

Current community focus

  • Sanitized technical case studies
  • Migration and recovery notes
  • Security boundary reviews
  • Reference implementations where safe
  • Clear separation of experiments and validated work
